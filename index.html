So Who Am I?

I am an independent designer, drop-shipper and a shopify expert. I’ve been running two of my own e-commerce stores built on Shopify and also have helped three other stores to set up on the same platform. Other than that, I’ve been working as a freelancer on various other projects from designing a logo to handling their social media accounts and ads.
I’ve been into designing stuff full of aesthetics and building out brands for the last three years and my deep understanding of how to create an unmatched brand identity has always proven to be one of the ideal stepping stones for not only creating a great brand, but distributing it to the right audience.
All in all, Visually Pleasing Stuff Sells, and I Create Just That.
